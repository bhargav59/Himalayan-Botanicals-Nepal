Company Overview
Himalayan Botanicals Nepal is a Nepal-based exporter specializing in premium Himalayan medicinal
herbs and natural ingredients for international wholesale buyers.


The company sources products directly from trusted collectors, farmers, and processing partners across
Nepal and supplies them to importers, manufacturers, distributors, and private-label brands worldwide.


The business focuses on transparency, traceability, laboratory-tested quality, sustainable sourcing, and
long-term partnerships.


This website is designed to establish credibility, generate qualified wholesale inquiries, and position the
company as a trusted export partner.




Business Model
This is a Business-to-Business (B2B) wholesale export company.


It is not:

Content Guidelines
Use factual language.


Avoid exaggerated claims.


Never promise medical outcomes.


Never claim government approvals that do not exist.


Do not use excessive marketing language such as:


"Best in the world"


"Guaranteed miracle"


"100% cure"


Instead, focus on measurable specifications, sourcing practices, and export capabilities.

Success Criteria
The project is successful when:


International buyers trust the company within the first two minutes of visiting the website.


Visitors can quickly understand the company's capabilities, products, and export process.


The website consistently generates qualified wholesale inquiries.


The codebase remains clean, scalable, and production-ready throughout development.




Instructions for Codex
Before implementing any new feature:


    1. Read this document.
    2. Review the PRD.
    3. Reuse existing architecture whenever possible.
    4. Do not introduce unnecessary dependencies.
    5. Prioritize scalability and maintainability.
    6. Keep the design consistent with the established design system.
    7. Never sacrifice accessibility or performance for visual effects.
    8. If a requirement is unclear, implement the solution that best supports a premium B2B export
       website rather than a consumer e-commerce site.
