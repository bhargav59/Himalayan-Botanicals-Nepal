Purpose
This document defines how all written content for Himalayan Botanicals Nepal should be created.


Every page, heading, paragraph, product description, button, form, and article must follow this guide.


If there is any conflict between creative writing and this document, this guide takes precedence.




Primary Objective
The website exists to build trust with international B2B buyers.


The goal is not to "sell products."


The goal is to reduce buyer uncertainty until they feel comfortable requesting a quotation.


Every sentence should contribute toward that objective.

Tone
Use factual, respectful, and concise language.


Avoid unnecessary adjectives.


Prioritize clarity over persuasion.


Every statement should be believable and verifiable.




Writing Style
Write in plain international English.


Avoid slang.


Avoid jargon unless necessary.


Explain technical terms when they first appear.


Use active voice whenever possible.


Keep paragraphs short.


Prefer bullet lists for specifications.

Medical & Regulatory Compliance
Products such as Shilajit, Ashwagandha, Tulsi, Brahmi, and other botanicals must not be marketed with
unverified medical claims.


Instead of claiming therapeutic effects, focus on:


Botanical identity


Traditional use


Origin


Processing


Quality


Laboratory testing


Packaging


Specifications


Export readiness


If discussing traditional use, clearly indicate that it is based on historical or traditional practices and not
intended as a medical claim.
