Purpose
This document defines every Hero component used throughout the Himalayan Botanicals Nepal
website.


A Hero component is the first meaningful visual and informational section on a page.


Its purpose is to immediately communicate:


        • Who we are
        • What we supply
        • Why buyers should trust us
        • What action they should take next

Every Hero must encourage visitors to continue exploring or request a quotation.




Hero Philosophy
The Hero should communicate:


Professionalism


Trust


Authenticity


Premium Quality


Export Readiness


Natural Origin


International Standards


Transparency

                                                  18
Instructions for Codex
Before creating or modifying a Hero:


    1. Use the appropriate Hero type.
    2. Keep messaging concise and factual.
    3. Prioritize trust over marketing language.
    4. Display one primary and one secondary CTA only.
    5. Use authentic imagery and verified information.
    6. Follow the layout hierarchy and spacing system.
    7. Ensure accessibility and performance.
    8. If uncertain, choose the simpler layout that communicates credibility most effectively.
