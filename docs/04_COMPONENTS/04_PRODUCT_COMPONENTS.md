Purpose
This document defines every product-related component used throughout the website.


Product components are the most important conversion components in the application.


Their responsibility is to reduce buyer uncertainty and encourage qualified wholesale inquiries.


Every component should answer one of these questions:


What is the product?


Where is it sourced?


Can I trust its quality?


Can this company export it?


How do I buy it?




Product Philosophy
Products should be presented as industrial ingredients.


Never present them as retail consumer products.


The design should resemble professional ingredient manufacturers.


Every product page should communicate:


Professionalism


Scientific accuracy

Instructions for Codex
Before creating or modifying a product component


    1. Follow the product hierarchy.
    2. Reuse existing product components.
    3. Present products as professional industrial ingredients.
    4. Never fabricate specifications or certifications.
    5. Keep technical information structured and easy to scan.
    6. Prioritize buyer confidence over promotional language.
    7. Ensure every product page ends with a clear Request Quote CTA.
